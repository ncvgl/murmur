No installation. No backend. No API costs. No data leaving your computer.

### How it works

Moonshine v1, a state-of-the-art speech model from ex-Googlers, runs locally in your browser.

It listens to your mic + speakers. Transcribes everything. Keeps it on your device.

### Why this matters

Companies charge $10-30/month for meeting transcription because they run expensive APIs on their servers.

This costs $0 because there's no server. The AI runs on YOUR device.

### The tradeoff

~90% accuracy (vs 95% for cloud services). But it's private, offline, and free forever.
